Tell Me More

Goal: re-inventing money

TU Delft is home to the one of the largest research groups on blockchain technology. Our work combines running code with novel approaches to broaden and strengthen the cybercurrency field. We aim to provide a secure, scalable, cybercurrency without forks and without the need for confirmations.

Early Pioneers

Back in 2007 the lab introduced the 'bandwidth as a currency' vision together with Harvard University, full Internet-deployment and unbounded scalability. See BBC News coverage.

Wikipedia

Another milestone for the lab was the cooperation with Wikipedia.org website, further expanding our "bandwidth as a currency" work. Within this partnership we deployed a browser plugin which enables users to donate bandwidth to the Wikipedia website.

Web of Trust

Our research challenge is moving beyond the classical wasteful "proof-of-something" approach.
